Game Flow

Now that you've got your tools and (assuming) your friends, it's time to begin the game!
The game flow is in a few phase:

Pre-Phase: Role time!

#image here
Before you start the game, you distribute your roles!

Note: This would be pretty hard physically... if you didn't have this site! (again, self advertising, but still)

There are 3 roles in this game:

Assassin

#image here

"Cash ain't heaven, but it sure looks like it!"
There's only 1 goal for this role: kill your target.

So be patient, be manipulative, or be aggressive. Just to whatever it takes to get the gun primed.

Spy

#image here

"He could be in this very room!"
Your goal is to kill the Assassin and protect the priests.

And to do that, you're the only other role with access to the gun. Though how you can do that in a room of confused idiots and a killer is up to you.

Monk

#image here

"...May Buddha help us."
Speaking of confused idiots, these are the Monks! The normal people of the game.
Unfortunately, as pacafists, they abhor using guns. But they CAN hold it.

Not much to say for this role except: Survive!


Note: There can only be 1 Assassin and Spy. Anyone else has to be Monks.

Now without further ado, the game loop begins!

Phase 1: Talk time!

#image here
Spin the gun to see who gets the gun first, and start the timer (default is 30 seconds).

This is the time to figure out who's who, and who should the get the gun. Afterwards...

Phase 2: Pass time!

#image here
The person who has the gun has to pass it onto someone else, they can choose whoever.

Though, if everyone deems that person untrustworthy, they can veto it and give it to someone else! Just make sure you choose correctly, or else it'll be a bad...

Phase 3: Pray time!

#image here
Once that person has the gun, you pray and see if he shoots or not! Just pray that he'll miss!

Keep in mind though, Monks are pacafists, so they can't use the gun. While the Assassin and Spy can only use the gun after 3 touches.
So pay close attention, and count how many times anyone's held the gun, it could save your life!
